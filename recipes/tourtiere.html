Tourtiere

What Is Tourtiere?

Tourtiere is a dish that consists of a cut of beef that is coated in paté, wrapped in puff pastry, and baked until the pastry is a beautiful golden brown. The classic English dish is named after Arthur Wellesley, 1st Duke of Wellington.

Ingredients

  • beef
  • butter
  • onions

Steps

  1. Cover the beef with butter and bake until browned.
  2. Sauté the onions and mushrooms in butter. Mix the paté with butter and seasonings.
  3. Spread the paté mixture over the beef and top with the veggie mixture.
